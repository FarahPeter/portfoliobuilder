About FolioForge

Made by a builder, for the next ones

FolioForge started with a simple observation: the students doing the most interesting work often have the least visible presence online. A GitHub full of gold, a Behance nobody visits, a resume that undersells everything.

So we build the site you'd make for yourself if you had the weeks to spare — hand-crafted, animated, fast, on a domain with your name on it. And because it's run lean on our own infrastructure, it costs $5 a month instead of $50.

FAQ

Questions, answered

How fast will my portfolio be ready?

Most sites go live within about 48 hours of our first chat. Complex sites (lots of projects, custom interactions) can take a few days — we'll give you a timeline up front.

Do I really get my own domain?

Yes. We register a domain of your choice (like yourname.com) and it's registered to you. If you ever leave, the domain and your content go with you.

How do payments work?

Simple and personal: once we agree on your plan in the chat, we send you a payment link or invoice. $5/month or $50/year — no hidden extras, cancel whenever.

Can I update my site later?

Anytime. Message us in the chat with your new project, internship or thesis and we'll update your site — it's included in every plan.

What if I already have a domain?

Even easier — we'll point it at your new site and handle all the DNS and SSL setup for you.
